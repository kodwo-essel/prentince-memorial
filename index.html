WHO WE ARE

A Preventive & Restorative Wellness Clinic

Prentice Memorial Clinic is a modern wellness and organ restoration center dedicated to helping individuals rebuild their health from the inside out. We are not a symptom-management facility. We are a structured, disciplined and patient-centered practice focused on restoring organ function, strengthening the body's systems and guiding long-term health transformation.

Led by Dr. Dwight Prentice, the clinic blends clinical assessment with practical daily lifestyle correction. Every patient receives a clear roadmap - not confusion, not guesswork. When you visit our clinic or our website, you experience clarity, order and direction.
